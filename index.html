system design

Inside a System I Built

A CV tells you what I did. This shows how I think. Below is the architecture of the LLM-powered e-commerce assistant, and the reasoning behind the key decisions.

Why RAG instead of fine-tuning?

Product catalogs and policies change daily. Retrieval keeps answers grounded in current data without retraining, and every answer can cite its source.

Why tool calling behind the API?

The model never touches the database. It requests actions like order lookup through validated, permission-checked backend functions, so the same security rules apply to AI and humans.

Why audit every interaction?

Coming from regulated government platforms, I treat AI output as a system action: logged, attributable, and reviewable when something goes wrong.
